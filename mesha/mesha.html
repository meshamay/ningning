The Scholars' Circle
RTU
The Rizal Technological College or RTU was established on July 11, 1969, upon the approval by the Secretary of Education in response to the request of the Provincial Board of Rizal, headed by then Governor Isidro S. Rodriguez, to put up a higher education institution in the Province of Rizal. It was first known as the College of Rizal. The College of Rizal opened its classes on July 14, 1969, with course offerings in business administration, education and liberal arts.
